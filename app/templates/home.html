WELCOME TO WISHLIST


This is a web based application, that allows its users to save items to their wish lists; from any website. Users should be able to select the image, they wish to use as the cover of their wish list item. User are also able to share their wishlists with other persons; this is done by entering the email address of the person they wish to send their wish list to.
